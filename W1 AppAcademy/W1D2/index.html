Kimchi Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • Kimchi
  • Rice
  • Carrots
  • Corn
  • Peas

Description

Classic Korean recipe, good for hangovers.

Wiki link


Directions

  1. First you steam the rice at some temperature for a certain period of time.
  2. Then turn your pan on to a high temperature, probably.
  3. Add oil to the pan and start stir frying your vegetables (carrtos, peas, and corn)
  4. Once they are mostly cooked, throw in a good portion of rice.
  5. When everything is almost about done, throw in the kimchi and toss everything around.
